About Me

The twenty-year-old writer, Kartik Patel hails from Palanpur, Gujarat. He is currently pursuing Chartered Accountancy course and B.Com from GLS University, Ahmedabad. He tried his hand at writing for the first time in the blog. He has a clear vision of how humanity lost in different forces which predominantly promote the division. In this book 'The Notion of Human Unifying', he attempted to clarify how dividing forces dig a big hole in Humanism and put a notion about human unification. He believes that each human need support and every human have an obligation to help others. Only human is competent for helping humanity. We need each other.
